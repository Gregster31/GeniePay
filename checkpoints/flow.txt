BULK PAY FLOW EXPLAINED:
- Contract: Singleton, no employee data stored.
- Employer: Prepares list off-chain, approves tokens, calls bulkPayERC20(token, addresses[], amounts[]).
- Employees: Receive tokens automatically; no interaction needed.
- Notes: Gas grows with employee count; employer pays all fees.

DB FLOW EXPLAINED:
GeniePay uses PostgreSQL via Supabase for persistent storage, with your wallet address as your identity, no account creation needed. 
How it works: When you connect your wallet, GeniePay silently authenticates you with Supabase using your wallet address as a deterministic identity. 
Supabase issues a JWT, and PostgreSQL's Row Level Security ensures you can only ever read and write your own employee data, 
enforced at the database level, not just in the frontend. Reconnect the same wallet on any device or browser and your entire team roster is there instantly.
